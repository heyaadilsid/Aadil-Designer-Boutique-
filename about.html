Our Story

A tailoring table in Ghumar Mandi.

Aadil Designer Boutique didn't start as a showroom — it started as a tailor's table in Ludhiana's oldest textile market, and it has stayed close to that root ever since.

Owner & Master Tailor

Shakeel Ahmed

Shakeel Ahmed built Aadil Designer Boutique on a simple standard: a garment isn't finished until it fits the person wearing it, not just the mannequin it was designed on. He personally oversees every measurement, every embroidery placement, and every final fitting that happens in the store.

Families across Ludhiana — from Sarabha Nagar to Model Town — come to Ghumar Mandi specifically for his eye on fit and his refusal to rush a bridal order, even during peak wedding season.

Rooted in Ghumar Mandi

Ludhiana's textile heart, still doing it by hand

Ghumar Mandi has been Ludhiana's textile and tailoring district for generations. Aadil Designer Boutique is part of that lineage — we don't import finished garments to relabel; every piece that leaves our store was cut and embroidered at this address.

That includes Punjab's own phulkari embroidery, worked into sarees and dupattas alongside the more familiar zardozi and gota-patti bridal techniques.
