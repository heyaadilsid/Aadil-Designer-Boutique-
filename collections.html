For the Ceremony

Bridal Lehengas

Our heaviest, most considered work — hand zardozi and gota-patti embroidery on silk and raw silk bases, built for the day you'll be photographed the most. Production typically takes 6–10 weeks depending on embroidery density, so we recommend starting your consultation as early as possible.

Colour, embroidery weight and dupatta drape are all decided together with you, not chosen off a fixed catalogue.

Enquire about Bridal Lehengas
Reception & Sangeet

Sarees

Draped and pre-stitched sarees with hand-worked borders and pallus — including Punjab's own phulkari embroidery alongside more traditional zardozi work. Fitted blouses are tailored to your measurements, not sold as a standard size.

A strong choice for guests who want the drama of a saree without managing the drape all evening.

Enquire about Sarees
Mehendi to Diwali

Anarkalis & Festive Wear

Lighter cottons, chanderi and soft silks for daytime functions — mehendi, haldi, and festivals like Diwali and Eid. Bright, comfortable, and built to be worn again well after the wedding season ends.

We deliberately keep embroidery lighter here — these are meant for hours of movement, not just photographs.
