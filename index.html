The Atelier

A tailor's shop first, a boutique second.

Aadil Designer Boutique sits in the textile heart of Ludhiana — Ghumar Mandi — where Shakeel Ahmed has spent years learning the difference between a garment that photographs well and one that survives a twelve-hour wedding day. Every order is measured, cut and finished on-site, not sent out to be assembled elsewhere.

We don't stock racks of ready-made pieces pretending to be bespoke. What you see in a consultation is what gets built for you — your colours, your fit, your embroidery density.
